# Best Chocolate Chip Cookies
The recipe is from my dad, and they are a favorite among my friends and family. The secret ingredient is the coconut! _Be warned_ though, these will fly off the plate!
##Ingredients
* Butter
* White Sugar
* Brown Sugar
* Eggs
* Vanilla
* Flour
* Baking Soda
* Salt
* Chocolate chips
* Oatmeal
* Coconut
##Directions
1. Preheat the oven to 325.
2. Beat the butter, sugar, eggs and vanilla together until creamy.
3. Mix together the flour, baking soda and salt in a seperate bowl.
4. Add flour mixture to batter slowly.
5. Stir in the chocolate chips, oatmeal, and coconut.
6. Bake for **10 minutes** or until golden brown.
